Sneak Peek into November

OBX Shrimp CookOff!

The 10th annual Shrimp CookOff is slated to be held at Ocean Boulevard Bistro & Martini Bar in Kitty Hawk, NC - from 12pm until 3pm on Sunday, November 3rd. This casual, fun competition has been a huge success since 2010. They will have 8-10 competing chefs from around the Outer Banks, cooking fresh, local shrimp in their favorite style of preparation. The cost per person is $20 at the door, or can be purchased in advance. This event benefits dolphin research.
